## ☁️ About Me

I'm **Akash** — a Cloud & Database Engineer with nearly **3 years** of hands-on experience designing and operating enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure at scale.

My world revolves around **cloud** — and I mean that deeply. I architect **Hub-and-Spoke topologies on OCI**, build **multi-zone resilient systems on AWS**, and engineer the infrastructure backbone that connects hybrid and multicloud environments handling **300TB+ of business-critical data**. I've led the migration of **22 on-premises databases** onto Oracle Exadata@GCP, cutting through complexity, latency, and cost at every turn.

On the cloud-native side, I live and breathe **Terraform** — defining entire environments as code, spinning up VPCs, compute, IAM policies, and security layers without touching a console. My CI/CD pipelines (powered by **GitHub Actions**) keep deployments fast, auditable, and reproducible.

Whether it's configuring **OCI FastConnect** for private multicloud peering, hardening **IAM vaults and secrets**, optimizing **Exadata and Autonomous Database** performance, or scripting Linux automation to shave hours off runbooks — I'm building infrastructure that doesn't just work, it *scales*, *heals*, and *stays secure*.

> *"Cloud is not the destination — it's the engine. I build the engine."*
